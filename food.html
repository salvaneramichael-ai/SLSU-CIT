About the Room

The Culinary Technology Laboratory provides a professional kitchen environment where students develop their cooking, baking, and food presentation skills. It is equipped with modern kitchen appliances and tools used in the hospitality and food service industry. The program focuses on culinary artistry, hygiene, and innovation in food preparation and management.
