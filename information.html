About the Room

The Information Technology Laboratory provides students with advanced training in programming, networking, and computer systems management. Equipped with modern PCs and networking equipment, the lab supports activities in web development, database systems, and cybersecurity. Students gain both theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience in the fast-evolving field of IT.
