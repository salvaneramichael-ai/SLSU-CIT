About the Room

The Print Media Technology Laboratory provides students with practical experience in printing, layout design, and digital prepress operations. It is equipped with offset printing machines, digital printers, and graphic workstations. Students learn the entire printing workflow from design and color management to finishing and publishing preparing them for careers in the print and publication industry.
