About Us

The College of Industrial Technology (CIT) is committed to providing quality education, fostering research, and developing professionals who are leaders in their fields. We pride ourselves in shaping future innovators with strong values and skills for nation-building.

Vision

We aspire to be a leading innovative, high-performing college that develops technologically proficient graduates.

Mission

Contribute in the skills development of the Filipino workers innovatively proficient, productive, and versatile in all aspects of technology evolution. Transform students with competence essentials for technological advancement.

Goals and Objectives

  1. Develop qualified students to be effective instruments of inquiry and research with a sense of responsibility and commitment for the improvement of the human condition physical, mental, and spiritual domains.
  2. Stimulate and maintain functional and productive activities in various disciplines.
  3. Acquire skills and leadership competence in various disciplines to accelerate community progress as trained professionals in government and non-government agencies.
  4. Adapt skills and internalize intellectual integrity, moral, cultural, and aesthetic values exemplifying ethical norms and professionalism.
