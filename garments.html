About the Room

The Apparel Technology Laboratory provides students with hands-on experience in garment design, pattern making, cutting, and sewing. It is equipped with industrial-grade sewing machines and pressing equipment that simulate a real production environment. Students learn how to design and produce apparel with precision, creativity, and attention to detail.
